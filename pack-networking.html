Networking & Professional Branding

Goal (≈10 hours)

Enhance your professional presence by polishing your online profiles, developing an elevator pitch and actively expanding your network. Learn how to connect authentically with others and maintain relationships.

Suggested tools:

  • LinkedIn or other professional networking platform
  • Email client and calendar
  • Templates for elevator pitches
  • Networking events or online communities
  • ChatGPT

Guided tasks

  1. Optimise your LinkedIn profile. Update your headline, summary, experience and skills sections to reflect your strengths and aspirations. Add a professional photo and customise your banner. Tip: Ask ChatGPT for suggestions on compelling phrases and keywords relevant to your industry.
  2. Craft an elevator pitch. Write a 30‑second script introducing yourself, your goals and what makes you unique. Practise reciting it aloud until it sounds natural. Record yourself and refine your wording and pacing. Tip: Tailor your pitch for different audiences (recruiters, peers, mentors).
  3. Connect with professionals. Identify three people in your field (alumni, peers or thought leaders) and send personalised connection requests or emails. Introduce yourself, mention a common interest or mutual connection and request a brief chat. Tip: Keep messages concise and genuine.
  4. Attend an event or join a community. Participate in at least one networking event, webinar or online forum. Engage by asking questions, sharing insights and following up with new contacts. Tip: Take notes on interesting conversations and plan follow‑up messages within a week.
  5. Reflect on your professional brand. Write a short reflection on what you learned about personal branding and networking. Describe actions you will take to nurture your network going forward. Tip: Ask ChatGPT for advice on maintaining connections over time.

Tip: Spread these activities over several days and schedule time for outreach. Don’t be discouraged by non‑responses—networking is a long‑term practice.

Need help getting started? Break each task down into small steps, explore free tutorials online and ask ChatGPT to explain unfamiliar concepts. Practise iteratively and document your progress as you go.

What to submit

  • Link to your updated LinkedIn profile
  • Your elevator pitch script (text or video)
  • Screenshots or copies of your outreach messages and any replies
  • A summary of the event or community engagement and your reflection
