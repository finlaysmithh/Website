Photo Editing & Typography

Goal (≈10 hours)

Capture and edit a cohesive photo series, experiment with typography and learn to balance text and imagery for maximum impact. This pack helps you tell visual stories with precision and style.

Suggested tools:

  • Camera or smartphone
  • Lightroom, Photoshop or GIMP
  • Typography resources (fonts & guides)
  • Inspiration sources (books, posters)
  • ChatGPT

Guided tasks

  1. Plan and shoot a photo series. Choose a theme (e.g., a day in the life, architecture, food) and plan at least six shots that tell a story. Consider lighting, composition and subject placement. Shoot your images, making notes on why you chose each composition. Tip: Use the rule of thirds and experiment with different angles or focal lengths.
  2. Edit your photos like a pro. Import your images into Lightroom, Photoshop or a free alternative. Adjust exposure, contrast and white balance, then crop and straighten. Apply selective edits to highlight the subject and remove distractions. Document each step and save before/after versions. Tip: Work with adjustment layers and masks to keep your edits non‑destructive.
  3. Create a typographic poster. Select one of your edited photos and design a poster that combines the image with a meaningful quote or title. Choose two harmonious fonts and carefully arrange the text to complement the image. Pay attention to hierarchy, alignment, spacing and contrast. Tip: Use online typography pairings or ChatGPT suggestions to choose fonts.
  4. Analyse real‑world typography. Collect three examples of typography you admire (book covers, magazine spreads, advertisements). For each, write a brief analysis explaining why the typeface, sizing, spacing and layout work well. Summarise the principles you can apply to your own designs. Tip: Look for examples with different moods to broaden your perspective.

Tip: Use available tutorials, sample projects or ask ChatGPT for guidance as you complete each task.

Need help getting started? Break each task down into small steps, explore free tutorials online and ask ChatGPT to explain unfamiliar concepts. Practise iteratively and document your progress as you go.

What to submit

  • Your photo series (original and edited versions)
  • Detailed edit notes or screenshots of your workflow
  • The finished typographic poster file
  • Analyses of three typography examples

Email your submission to Finlay@experience4grads.co.uk. Reviewers will respond within 48 hours. A certificate and LinkedIn endorsement will be awarded for work of sufficient quality.
