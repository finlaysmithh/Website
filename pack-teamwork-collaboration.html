Teamwork & Collaboration

Goal (≈10 hours)

Strengthen your ability to work effectively with others. You will practise planning a project, communicating clearly, dividing tasks and reflecting on team dynamics.

Suggested tools:

  • Document sharing (Google Docs, Office 365)
  • Project management tool (Trello, Asana)
  • Communication platform (Slack, Teams)
  • Video conferencing (Zoom, Google Meet)
  • ChatGPT

Guided tasks

  1. Plan a group project. Define a small project (e.g., organising a charity event or creating a guide). Draft a charter that includes goals, deliverables, roles and a timeline. Share your plan with at least one collaborator and agree on responsibilities. Tip: Use a project management tool to visualise tasks and deadlines.
  2. Host a team meeting. Schedule and lead a 30‑minute meeting (virtual or in person) to discuss progress. Prepare an agenda, take meeting notes and summarise decisions and action items. Tip: Practise active listening and ensure everyone has a chance to contribute.
  3. Collaborate on a shared document. Work with your collaborator on a shared file, using comments and suggestions to refine the content. Aim for a polished deliverable such as a presentation, article or guide. Tip: Resolve conflicts by focusing on the project goals and seeking consensus.
  4. Reflect on the teamwork experience. Write a short reflection assessing what went well and what could be improved. Consider communication, planning, role clarity and accountability. Tip: Ask ChatGPT how to navigate common collaboration challenges.

Tip: If you don’t have a collaborator, simulate one by switching perspectives or using ChatGPT to propose suggestions. Focus on clear communication and follow‑through.

Need help getting started? Break each task down into small steps, explore free tutorials online and ask ChatGPT to explain unfamiliar concepts. Practise iteratively and document your progress as you go.

What to submit

  • Your project charter and timeline
  • Meeting agenda, notes and action items
  • The final deliverable created collaboratively
  • A reflection on your teamwork experience
