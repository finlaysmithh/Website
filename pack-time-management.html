Time Management & Productivity

Goal (≈10 hours)

Adopt techniques and tools to organise your time, prioritise tasks and boost productivity. You will track how you spend your day, experiment with proven methods and design a realistic schedule for a personal project.

Suggested tools:

  • Calendar app (Google Calendar, Outlook)
  • To‑do or project board (Trello, Todoist)
  • Pomodoro timer (mobile app or online)
  • Time tracking tool (RescueTime, Toggl)
  • ChatGPT

Guided tasks

  1. Log your current time use. For one full day, record what you do each hour (work, study, breaks, leisure). At the end, categorise your activities and identify time sinks or patterns. Tip: Use a spreadsheet or time‑tracking app to visualise your data.
  2. Try a time management technique. Choose a method such as Pomodoro, the Eisenhower Matrix or Getting Things Done (GTD). Apply it for at least one day. Record how it affects your focus and energy levels. Tip: Ask ChatGPT for step‑by‑step instructions on your chosen method.
  3. Plan a personal project schedule. Select a small project (e.g., writing an article, building a website) and break it into tasks. Estimate the time required for each task and schedule them across your calendar over several days. Tip: Buffer extra time for unexpected delays and consider your peak productivity hours.
  4. Evaluate productivity tools. Test at least two apps or techniques for managing tasks (e.g., Trello vs. a simple to‑do list). Write a short comparison of their features, advantages and disadvantages. Tip: Consider ease of use, customisation and cross‑platform sync.
  5. Reflect on your experience. Summarise what you learned about your habits and time management. Describe which technique and tools worked best for you and how you plan to maintain productive routines. Tip: Ask ChatGPT for suggestions on overcoming common productivity hurdles.

Tip: Schedule your tasks across multiple days rather than cramming them into a single session. Use reminders and progress trackers to stay accountable.

Need help getting started? Break each task down into small steps, explore free tutorials online and ask ChatGPT to explain unfamiliar concepts. Practise iteratively and document your progress as you go.

What to submit

  • Your time log and analysis
  • A short report on the time management technique you tried
  • Your project plan and schedule
  • Comparison of productivity tools
  • A reflection summarising your findings and plans
