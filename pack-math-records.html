Mathematics & Accurate Record Keeping

Goal (≈10 hours)

Strengthen your numerical accuracy and design tools to ensure precise documentation in healthcare. These exercises develop essential maths skills and record‑keeping habits.

Suggested tools:

  • Excel or Google Sheets
  • Calculator
  • Word or document editor
  • ChatGPT

Guided tasks

  1. Build a dosage calculator. Create a spreadsheet that calculates medication dosages based on patient weight and prescribed dose per kilogram. Include at least ten different weights and use formulas (e.g., =weight*dosage_per_kg) to compute individual doses. Add conditional formatting to flag doses outside a safe range. Double‑check your formula results with a handheld calculator. Tip: Use data validation to prevent invalid weight entries.
  2. Design a comprehensive record template. Develop a digital or printable form for recording vital signs, medications administered, allergies and procedures. Use tables and clearly labelled sections to minimise errors. If you’re using Excel, freeze header rows and use drop‑down lists for common values. Tip: Look at examples of patient charts for inspiration and prioritise readability.
  3. Track fluid balance. Document fluid intake and output for a hypothetical patient over 24 hours. Record all sources (oral, IV, urine, drains, etc.) and compute total intake, total output and net balance in your spreadsheet. Insert a small bar chart to visualise the balance over time. Tip: Use SUM and SUMIF functions for totals and filter features to view specific data.
  4. Reflect on accuracy in practice. Write a 1–2 page reflection discussing why precise calculations and record keeping are critical to patient safety. Include at least one real‑world case where errors in dosing or documentation led to adverse outcomes and how such mistakes can be prevented. Tip: Use authoritative sources (e.g., clinical journals or safety reports) to support your points.

Tip: Use available tutorials, sample projects or ask ChatGPT for guidance as you complete each task.

Need help getting started? Break each task down into small steps, explore free tutorials online and ask ChatGPT to explain unfamiliar concepts. Practise iteratively and document your progress as you go.

What to submit

  • Your dosage spreadsheet with formulas and conditional formatting
  • The record‑keeping template you designed
  • Your fluid balance tracking sheet and accompanying chart
  • A reflective essay on the importance of accuracy in healthcare

Email all files to Finlay@experience4grads.co.uk. Submissions are reviewed within 48 hours. Successful work will earn a certificate and a LinkedIn endorsement.
