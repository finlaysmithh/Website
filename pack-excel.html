Excel & Advanced Spreadsheet Modelling

Goal (≈10 hours)

Master advanced Excel functions, build dynamic spreadsheets and develop data‑driven financial models to support decision‑making.

Suggested tools:

  • Excel
  • Sample datasets
  • Calculator
  • ChatGPT

Guided tasks

  1. Create a dynamic budgeting spreadsheet. Design a workbook to track monthly revenues and expenses. Use formulas such as SUM, IF, INDEX/MATCH or VLOOKUP to build a flexible model. Add data‑validation drop‑downs and conditional formatting to make the sheet user‑friendly. Include at least one chart (bar or line) to visualise trends. Tip: Ask ChatGPT for formula examples or troubleshooting if you get stuck.
  2. Explore pivot tables. Import a real or synthetic dataset (e.g., sales or survey data) and build pivot tables to summarise information by category, region and period. Experiment with calculated fields and slicers to view different perspectives. Export or capture screenshots of your most insightful tables. Tip: Use the PivotTable field list to drag and drop dimensions; double‑click values to drill down.
  3. Develop a three‑year forecasting model. Create a forecast for a hypothetical company, including assumptions for revenue growth, costs and tax rates. Use Excel’s Scenario Manager or Data Table tools to test best‑ and worst‑case scenarios. Present your forecasts using charts or a small dashboard. Tip: Use NPV, IRR or CHOOSE functions if needed and experiment with one‑ or two‑way sensitivity analysis.
  4. Document your process. Write a short report (1–2 pages) describing how you built your spreadsheets, the formulas and tools you used and any challenges you encountered. Reflect on how your model could be improved. Tip: A clear narrative and screenshots help reviewers understand your work.

Tip: Use available tutorials, sample projects or ask ChatGPT for guidance as you complete each task.

Need help getting started? Break each task down into small steps, explore free tutorials online and ask ChatGPT to explain unfamiliar concepts. Practise iteratively and document your progress as you go.

What to submit

  • Your Excel workbook(s) with budgeting, pivot table and forecasting models
  • A brief report documenting your approach and findings
  • Screenshots or exported views of your pivot tables and charts

Email your deliverables to Finlay@experience4grads.co.uk. Your work will be reviewed within 48 hours. If approved, you’ll receive a certificate and a LinkedIn endorsement.
