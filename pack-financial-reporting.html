Financial Reporting & Cost Accounting

Goal (≈10 hours)

Prepare accurate financial statements and perform cost analyses that inform strategic decisions.

Suggested tools:

  • Excel or Google Sheets
  • Accounting templates
  • Calculator
  • ChatGPT

Guided tasks

  1. Construct core financial statements. Using sample transaction data or your own business scenario, build an income statement and balance sheet in Excel. Categorise revenue, cost of goods sold, operating expenses, assets, liabilities and equity. Ensure that assets equal liabilities plus equity. Tip: You can find free templates online; ask ChatGPT if you need help classifying items.
  2. Calculate cost per unit. Collect or generate cost data for producing a product or delivering a service (materials, labour and overhead). Compute the cost per unit using job‑order or process costing methods and allocate overhead appropriately. Show your calculations clearly in your spreadsheet. Tip: Use formulas to automate repetitive calculations and consider using separate sheets for raw data and analysis.
  3. Perform variance analysis. Research material, labour and overhead variance concepts. Apply these to your cost data by comparing actual costs against budgeted or standard costs. Calculate price and efficiency variances and interpret what they indicate about performance. Present your results with tables or charts. Tip: Conditional formatting in Excel can highlight significant variances.
  4. Write a financial narrative. Summarise your findings in a concise report. Discuss the company’s profitability and liquidity, highlight key cost drivers, and explain the reasons behind any significant variances. Include charts or graphs to illustrate trends and support your discussion. Tip: Keep the narrative structured with sections like “Overview,” “Analysis,” and “Recommendations.”

Tip: Use available tutorials, sample projects or ask ChatGPT for guidance as you complete each task.

Need help getting started? Break each task down into small steps, explore free tutorials online and ask ChatGPT to explain unfamiliar concepts. Practise iteratively and document your progress as you go.

What to submit

  • Your Excel workbook with financial statements, cost per unit calculations and variance analysis
  • A brief report summarising your analysis and conclusions
  • Any charts or graphs used to illustrate results
