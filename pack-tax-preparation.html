Tax Preparation & Planning

Goal (≈10 hours)

Understand your jurisdiction’s tax system, practise completing a tax return and explore strategies for lawful tax planning.

Suggested tools:

  • Government tax resources (HMRC, IRS etc.)
  • Spreadsheet software
  • Tax calculators
  • ChatGPT

Guided tasks

  1. Research the tax system. Choose a country (ideally your own) and summarise its income tax brackets, deductions, allowances and credits for individuals and small businesses. Highlight any recent changes or common pitfalls. Present your findings in a concise report or table. Tip: Use official government websites and ChatGPT to clarify terminology.
  2. Prepare a sample tax return. Create fictional financial data for a sole trader or freelancer (income, expenses, donations). Complete a full tax return in a spreadsheet or by using free trial software. Show how you calculate taxable income, national insurance/self‑employment tax and applicable credits or reliefs. Tip: Document your assumptions and link cells to avoid manual errors.
  3. Identify tax planning strategies. Research and describe at least three legitimate strategies to reduce taxable income (e.g., pension contributions, capital allowances, timing of expenses, choosing between cash and accrual accounting). Explain how each strategy works and when it is appropriate. Tip: ChatGPT can help explain concepts like capital allowances and carry‑forward losses.
  4. Create a tax compliance calendar. Draft a calendar that lists key filing and payment deadlines for individuals and small businesses (quarterly payments, VAT returns, annual self‑assessment, corporation tax). Include reminders a few weeks before each due date. You can use a spreadsheet or digital calendar. Tip: Colour‑code different tax types to make the calendar easy to read.

Tip: Use available tutorials, sample projects or ask ChatGPT for guidance as you complete each task.

Need help getting started? Break each task down into small steps, explore free tutorials online and ask ChatGPT to explain unfamiliar concepts. Practise iteratively and document your progress as you go.

What to submit

  • Your tax system summary report
  • The completed sample tax return (spreadsheet or exported PDF)
  • A document outlining your tax planning strategies
  • Your tax compliance calendar

Email all files to Finlay@experience4grads.co.uk. Your submission will be reviewed within 48 hours. If approved, you’ll receive a certificate and a LinkedIn endorsement.
