Team Coordination & Cultural Competency

Goal (≈10 hours)

Develop leadership, communication and cultural awareness skills essential for coordinating healthcare teams. Through these tasks you’ll design practical plans and reflect on your own leadership style.

Suggested tools:

  • Healthcare communication frameworks (SBAR, ISBAR)
  • Presentation or diagramming software
  • Mindfulness apps or stress management resources
  • Leadership literature
  • ChatGPT

Guided tasks

  1. Create a communication plan. Draft a plan detailing how information should be shared within a healthcare team across a shift. Include briefings, handover protocols, debriefings and escalation procedures. Use a diagram or flowchart to illustrate the flow of information and specify tools such as SBAR or digital communication platforms. Tip: Consider how to handle unexpected events and maintain confidentiality.
  2. Design a cultural competency workshop. Outline a short training session that promotes cultural awareness and sensitivity among colleagues. Specify learning objectives, interactive activities (e.g., case studies, role‑play) and resources. Describe how you would measure the workshop’s effectiveness through feedback or pre‑/post‑tests. Tip: Incorporate local demographic data to make the content relevant.
  3. Propose a stress management programme. Develop a programme to support staff wellbeing, including techniques such as mindfulness exercises, peer‑support groups and structured debriefings after critical incidents. Explain how you would encourage participation and evaluate the programme’s impact on staff morale and patient care. Tip: Use evidence from research on burnout prevention.
  4. Reflect on leadership styles. Compare at least three leadership approaches (e.g., transformational, servant, transactional, situational) and discuss their suitability in healthcare settings. Describe which style resonates with you and how you would apply it when leading a team. Provide examples of behaviours you would adopt. Tip: Use ChatGPT to generate scenarios illustrating each style.

Tip: Use available tutorials, sample projects or ask ChatGPT for guidance as you complete each task.

Need help getting started? Break each task down into small steps, explore free tutorials online and ask ChatGPT to explain unfamiliar concepts. Practise iteratively and document your progress as you go.

What to submit

  • Your communication plan with diagram
  • The cultural competency workshop outline
  • A stress management programme proposal
  • A reflective essay comparing leadership styles

Send your deliverables to Finlay@experience4grads.co.uk. Submissions will be reviewed within 48 hours. Successful participants will receive a certificate and a LinkedIn endorsement.
