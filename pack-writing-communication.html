Writing & Email Etiquette

Goal (≈10 hours)

Craft clear, professional communications including emails, reports and cover letters, while mastering tone and structure.

Suggested tools:

  • Word processor (Google Docs, Microsoft Word)
  • Grammar checker (Grammarly or equivalent)
  • Email client
  • ChatGPT

Guided tasks

  1. Research email etiquette. Compile a checklist of key elements for professional emails (subject line, greeting, tone, signature). Write two sample emails: one requesting a meeting and one following up after an interview. Tip: Ask ChatGPT to review your drafts and suggest improvements.
  2. Write a cover letter. Draft a one‑page cover letter for a job in your desired field. Highlight your achievements using the STAR method (Situation, Task, Action, Result). Edit and polish your letter using ChatGPT or a grammar checker. Tip: Keep paragraphs concise and tailor the letter to the job description.
  3. Summarise an article. Choose a recent industry article and write a 500‑word report summarising its main points. Include an introduction, body and conclusion, and use headings to organise the sections. Tip: Outline your report before writing and cite any sources appropriately.
  4. Reflect on communication styles. Write a short paragraph comparing casual messaging (texts, chat) with formal business writing. Describe how you’ll adapt your style for different audiences. Tip: Consider tone, vocabulary and structure when switching contexts.

Tip: Use available templates and ask ChatGPT for examples as you complete these tasks.

Need help getting started? Break each task down into small steps, explore free tutorials online and ask ChatGPT to explain unfamiliar concepts. Practise iteratively and document your progress as you go.

What to submit

  • Your email etiquette checklist and two sample emails
  • Your tailored cover letter
  • The 500‑word article summary
  • Your reflection on communication styles
