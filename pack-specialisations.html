Specialisations in Healthcare

Goal (≈10 hours)

Gain insights into different healthcare specialisations—pediatrics, oncology, geriatrics and neonatal care. By comparing these fields, you’ll develop a nuanced understanding of patient needs and craft a comprehensive care plan.

Suggested tools:

  • Medical textbooks & journal articles
  • Spreadsheet or table editor
  • Care plan templates
  • ChatGPT

Guided tasks

  1. Compare specialisations. Research pediatrics, oncology, geriatrics and neonatal care. For each, summarise the primary clinical focus, common conditions, typical treatment approaches and any unique psychosocial considerations. Present your findings in a comparative table or matrix for easy reference. Tip: Note any age‑specific medication considerations or common team members involved.
  2. Analyse patient needs. Choose one specialisation that interests you and list the physical, psychological and social needs of typical patients in that field. Explain why these needs differ from those of the general population. Tip: Consider factors such as developmental stage, family involvement and end‑of‑life considerations.
  3. Develop a care plan. Using your chosen specialisation, create a hypothetical care plan for a patient. Include assessment data, nursing and medical interventions, patient education, goals and evaluation criteria. Format the plan using a template or design your own. Tip: Ensure your plan includes multidisciplinary collaboration (e.g., physiotherapy, nutrition, social work).
  4. Reflect on specialised practice. Write a reflective essay discussing the challenges and rewards of working in specialised healthcare. Address required training or certifications, collaboration with specialists and strategies for continued professional development. Tip: Consider how technological advances are changing care in your chosen specialisation.

Tip: Use available tutorials, sample projects or ask ChatGPT for guidance as you complete each task.

Need help getting started? Break each task down into small steps, explore free tutorials online and ask ChatGPT to explain unfamiliar concepts. Practise iteratively and document your progress as you go.

What to submit

  • The comparative table summarising four specialisations
  • A detailed analysis of patient needs for one speciality
  • Your completed care plan document
  • A reflective essay on specialised practice

Email your deliverables to Finlay@experience4grads.co.uk. Expect a response within 48 hours. If your work meets the criteria, you’ll receive a certificate and a LinkedIn endorsement.
