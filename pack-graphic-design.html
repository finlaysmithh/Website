Graphic Design & Adobe Creative Suite

Goal (≈10 hours)

Develop core design principles and practise using industry‑standard tools such as Illustrator, InDesign and Photoshop—or their free equivalents. By the end, you’ll have a mini portfolio of professional‑quality pieces.

Suggested tools:

  • Adobe Illustrator / Inkscape
  • Adobe InDesign / Scribus
  • Adobe Photoshop / GIMP
  • Colour palette generators
  • ChatGPT

Guided tasks

  1. Create a brand identity. Choose a fictional company or cause. Research its target audience and values, then design a logo using vector software. Develop a cohesive colour palette and select two complementary typefaces. Write a short rationale explaining your design decisions. Tip: Mood boards and palette generators (Coolors) can help you explore visual directions.
  2. Design a promotional poster. Using your logo and colour scheme, create a poster or flyer to advertise the brand. Pay attention to layout grid, hierarchy and typography. Include at least one custom illustration or icon. Document your process, noting how you apply design principles such as balance and contrast. Tip: Use the rule of thirds and adjust kerning/leading for professional polish.
  3. Enhance a photograph. Select a stock or original photo and perform a series of edits: correct exposure and colour, remove distractions, add subtle effects and integrate text. Keep track of each adjustment layer to demonstrate non‑destructive editing. Tip: Use layer masks and adjustment layers in Photoshop or GIMP to maintain flexibility.
  4. Assemble a mini portfolio. Compile your logo, poster and edited photo into a cohesive presentation. Arrange the work on slides or a simple webpage and write a reflection on what you learned about design. Describe how you would improve future projects based on feedback. Tip: Consider including before/after images to show progress.

Tip: Use available tutorials, sample projects or ask ChatGPT for guidance as you complete each task.

Need help getting started? Break each task down into small steps, explore free tutorials online and ask ChatGPT to explain unfamiliar concepts. Practise iteratively and document your progress as you go.

What to submit

  • Your logo and rationale document
  • The finished poster file
  • The edited photograph with a list of edits
  • A mini portfolio or presentation and reflection

Submit your work to Finlay@experience4grads.co.uk. Submissions will be reviewed within 48 hours. If the quality meets the standard, you’ll receive a certificate and a LinkedIn endorsement.
