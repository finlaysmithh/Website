Compliance, Audit & Portfolio Management

Goal (≈10 hours)

Understand regulatory requirements, design robust audit processes and build a small investment portfolio while ensuring compliance.

Suggested tools:

  • Official regulatory guidance (e.g., FCA, SEC, GDPR)
  • Spreadsheet software
  • Charting tool (Excel, Google Sheets)
  • ChatGPT

Guided tasks

  1. Research industry regulations. Choose a sector such as finance, healthcare or technology. Summarise the key regulations and standards that govern operations (e.g., SOX, HIPAA, MiFID). Explain why they exist and what penalties apply for non‑compliance. Tip: Use official regulator websites and summarise your findings in tables or bullet points for clarity.
  2. Create an internal control checklist. Draft a list of at least ten control activities (e.g., segregation of duties, access controls, reconciliation procedures) relevant to your chosen sector. For each control, describe its purpose, how it can be monitored, and what documentation is required. Tip: Organise the checklist by process (finance, HR, IT) and reference any applicable regulations.
  3. Develop a high‑level audit plan. For a fictional company in your sector, outline an audit plan covering objectives, scope, key risks, procedures, sampling methods and reporting. Use diagrams or flowcharts to illustrate the audit process and timeline. Tip: Refer to professional auditing standards for inspiration and ask ChatGPT for examples of audit objectives.
  4. Construct a sample investment portfolio. Select five assets (stocks, bonds, ETFs, mutual funds) that align with a basic investment policy. Document your rationale for each selection (e.g., risk tolerance, diversification). Create a table showing expected return, risk level and regulatory considerations. Describe how you would monitor the portfolio’s performance and maintain compliance with the investment policy. Tip: Use simple metrics like beta or yield, and build a chart to visualise allocation.

Tip: Use available tutorials, sample projects or ask ChatGPT for guidance as you complete each task.

Need help getting started? Break each task down into small steps, explore free tutorials online and ask ChatGPT to explain unfamiliar concepts. Practise iteratively and document your progress as you go.

What to submit

  • Your research summary on regulations
  • An internal control checklist
  • The audit plan document or presentation
  • Your portfolio analysis, including charts/tables
